Some Info And Introduction To FFXIV

Final Fantasy XIV: A Realm Reborn, or Final Fantasy 14 if you will, is an MMORPG made by Square Enix (SE), and it originally came out it 2013 for PC and PS3, and later on the PS4 and PS5.

It has 5 Expansions, the latest being Endwalker, which has received Universal acclaim by critics.

There is also a free trial for the game, where you can play from A realm Reborn (ARR) to Heavensward, and you can get up to level 60, which is a pretty good offer from SE, as originally it was up only to level 30 and only ARR.

Content wise there is a lot of different things you can do, like dungeons, trials and raids for battle content.

Dungeons are 4 person instanced duties, where you go in and fight groups of enemies, and a couple of Bosses, which are powerful enemies in-between each section of the dungeon.

Trials are also an instanced duty for 4-8 players, depending on the Trial. The objective in these duties is to defeat the Boss. Trials are usually unlocked through the main quest-line.

Raids is yet another type of instanced content for parties of 8 players, and is very similar to Trials where you have to defeat a Boss to progress, though unlike Trials they come in a series of 4 parts, and also usually has its own story which usually has very little to do with the main quest-line.

There is also a lot of other content to discover so I highly recommend trying the game out, as it is very fun game.

My Own Experience With FFXIV

I've been playing ffxiv for over 8 years, and i have had a lot of fun, both playing solo and with friends. Its a great community to be a part of.

I'm also a member of a Free Company, shortened to FC in most cases, and an FC is something similar to guilds in other games.

Being a part of an FC has a lot of advantages, like buffs, stables for your Chocobo for example, not to mention the social aspect of course, like events and content runs.

The FC I'm a part of is called Gilgamesh, shortened GIL, and it has a great community, with very helpful leaders and officers, annual events and great people.

I'm very happy to be part of GIL, made some friends there and I wouldn't have as much fun in the without them.

The Harder Stuff

I have also done some of the harder content and, as of writing this, I'm in a static, for handling said harder content such as Savages and Extremes, but mainly Savages.

Extremes are harder versions of the Trials, with some different mechanics compared to the normal version, though you can do this content by looking

Savages are a harder version of raids, with different and harder mechanics, and is best handled with a static.

It also helps having a discord for communication purposes, like call-outs and for discussing mechanics, cause you won't have much time to type anything when the fight has started.

some of the hardest content in the games is something called Ultimates, but this is something i haven't tried myself yet, i have just heard that its extremely hard to do.
